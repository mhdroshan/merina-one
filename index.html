Marina One Kochi – Luxury Waterfront Apartments :– Experience Iconic Living in Kerala’s Most Prestigious Address

Marina One Kochi is a premium waterfront residential project located at the heart of Marine Drive – the most sought-after location in Kochi. Jointly developed by Sobha Limited and Puravankara, this world-class development spans 16.7 acres of serene coastal land, offering 3, 3.5, and 4 BHK luxury apartments with breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea.
